# Phishing Email Identification

## Sample Email

Subject: Urgent Account Verification Required

Dear Customer,

Your bank account has been temporarily suspended.  
Click the link below immediately to verify your account.

http://secure-bank-verification-login.com

Failure to verify within 24 hours will result in account closure.

---

## Analysis

Indicators of phishing:

1. Generic greeting "Dear Customer"
2. Urgent message
3. Suspicious link
4. Request for sensitive information

Conclusion: This email is likely a phishing attempt.
